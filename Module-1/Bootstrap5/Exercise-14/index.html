Exercise 14: Display & Visibility

📱 Mobile only (d-block d-md-none): You are on a small screen.
💻 Tablet only (d-none d-md-block d-lg-none): You are on a medium screen.
🖥️ Desktop only (d-none d-lg-flex): You are on a large screen.
How it works:
  • d-none — hidden on all screens
  • d-block — shown as block
  • d-md-block — shown as block at md (≥768px) and above
  • d-lg-flex — shown as flex at lg (≥992px) and above
  • d-none d-md-block → hidden on mobile, shown on tablet+
