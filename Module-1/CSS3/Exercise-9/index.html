🎵 Annual Music Festival Returns to City Park

After a two-year hiatus, the beloved Community Music Festival is making a spectacular comeback this summer at City Park Amphitheater. Organizers have lined up over 20 local and regional bands to perform across two stages.

The festival will feature food stalls from local restaurants, artisan craft booths, and a dedicated kids' zone with activities for children of all ages. Entry is free for children under 12, and tickets for adults are priced affordably at just ₹200.

Volunteers are also being sought to help manage the event. Interested residents can sign up through the Community Portal or visit the city council office before June 10th.

"We are thrilled to bring the community together again through the power of music," said event organizer Priya Sharma. "This year will be the biggest festival we've ever organized, and we can't wait to share it with everyone."
